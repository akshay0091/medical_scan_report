Tuberculosis Checking

You have to uplod the X -ray .X-ray of the chest are useful in the examination of Tuberculosis. Tuberculosis (TB) is a potentially serious infectious disease that mainly affects the lungs. The bacteria that cause tuberculosis are spread from person to person through tiny droplets released into the air via coughs and sneezes.


There are two kinds of tests used to detect TB bacteria in the body: the TB skin test (TST) and TB blood tests . A positive TB skin test or TB blood test only tells that a person has been infected with TB bacteria.
